NBA Analytics

The keys to winning basketball in today's modern NBA.

My team and I analyzed data from the 2014 - 2016 NBA seasons to find correlations between our own generated team statistics and wins. We specifically looked at the most winningest team of the past three seasons, the Golden State Warriors, with the rest of league. Based off our analysis, we came to the conclusion that:

  • 1. Ball movement correlates with winning.
  • 2. The faster the pace of play, the more opportunities for 3 point shots, rebounds and assists.
  • 3. Defensive rebounds leads to more possessions, offensive opportunities, and points which ultimately leads to more wins.

  • Date: December 2017
  • Category: Data Analysis, Data Collection, Data Manipulation, Data Visualization

For more inforation, please view the full project on GitHub.
